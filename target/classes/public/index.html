REST API routes that can be opened in the browser:

  • /gms/{sku} --- RequestMethod.GET --- List the price and details of requested sku.
  • /gms --- RequestMethod.POST --- Create a price details for a particular sku.
  • /gms/{sku} --- RequestMethod.DELETE --- Delete the price document of requested sku.
Sample price document: { "sku": "1234", "price": 10, "currency": "INR", "updated": "2016-01-01 10:10:10", "description": "Smart watch", "category": "Electronics" "type": "gmprice" }
